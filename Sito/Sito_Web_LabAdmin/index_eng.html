What is about

Labadmin is an hardware and software solution that permit to assign to users a space, a username and an NFC tag.
At the same time the system also provides a chat service that allows users to communicate through the system, as well as the ability to monitor the behavior of the machines in terms of predictive maintenance, consumption management and calculation of the economic margin useful for the maintenance of all the environment. With the Tag or code the user can:

Access

You will be allowed to enter the available spaces in order to work with our machines to your project

Activate

It will be possible to reserve, activate or access the machines within the facilities that use LabAdmin

Credits

Check and earn the credits by participating in some jobs and activities for the use of the machines
